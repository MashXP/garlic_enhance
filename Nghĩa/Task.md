# Nghĩa's Quality Assessment Workflow (Project Garlic 2.0)
**Modified:** 2026-04-14 | **Lead:** Nghĩa

**Ultimate Goal:** Physically and chemically validate the "Synergy Index" by comparing seed garlic (*tỏi giống*) baseline data with final harvested yield.

---

## 🟡 Phase 0: Hybrid Baseline Protocol (Pre-Planting)
*Objective: Create a comprehensive "Quality Profile" for the starting material.*

### Step 0.1: Destructive Analysis (Representative Subset)
Perform full chemical profiling on a 10% sacrificial subset of the seed stock to establish the **Theoretical Population Baseline**.
- [ ] **Sample Prep**: Use the **80% Ethanol + Probe Sonication** method (optimized for Alliin preservation).
- [ ] **Establish "Golden Indices" Baseline**:
    - [ ] **Brix (TSS)**: Use handheld refractometer to establish the initial sugar density mean.
    - [ ] **TPC (Total Phenolic Content)**: Coordinate access to the **UV-Vis Spectrophotometer** at the Uni Lab; calibrate the Folin-Ciocalteu assay.
    - [ ] **Allicin Potential**: Perform initial water-based extraction baseline.

### Step 0.2: Non-Destructive Profiling (Individual Tracking)
Record physical data for the exact cloves intended for planting.
- [ ] **Unique ID Assignment**: Label each planting pot to correlate with specific seed data.
- [ ] **Physical Fingerprinting**:
    - [ ] Record Individual Clove Weight (g).
    - [ ] Measure individual clove volume via water displacement (optional but recommended for density calculation).
- [ ] **Morphological Registry**: Take high-res macro photos of seed cloves for future structural comparison.

---

## 🟠 Phase 1: Growth Monitoring (In-Situ)
*Objective: Correlate mid-growth health with final quality.*

- [ ] **Weekly Non-Destructive Checks**:
    - [ ] Leaf Count and Plant Height.
    - [ ] **Chlorophyll Index**: If SPAD equipment is accessible, measure leaf greenness to correlate with nitrogen uptake.
- [ ] **Uni Lab Coordination**: Ensure UV-Vis equipment is booked for the final "Assessable Stage" window.

---

## 🔴 Phase 2: Assessable Stage (Harvest & Comparison)
*Objective: Quantify the improvement delta.*

### Step 2.1: Final Quality Analysis (Destructive)
- [ ] **Harvest Sampling**: Harvest 10 bulbs per experimental group (Total $n=40$) as per `Trial_Design.md`.
- [ ] **Execute Split-Extraction Strategy**:
    - [ ] **UAE (Water)**: For maximum Allicin yield assessment.
    - [ ] **UAE (75% Water / 25% Acetone)**: For maximum TPC recovery.
- [ ] **UV-Vis Quantification**: Run spectrophotometry trials on all 4 groups ($C-$, $T1$, $T2$, $T3$).

### Step 2.2: The Synergy Index Calculation
- [ ] **Growth Correlation**: Cross-reference final bulb weight with the Phase 0.2 individual seed weights.
- [ ] **Calculate the Delta**:
    - [ ] Compare harvested quality against the Phase 0.1 Population Baseline.
    - [ ] Prove if $T3 > (T1 + T2 - C-)$ for Brix and TPC.

---

- [ ] **The "Measurement Cookbook"**: A finalized step-by-step PDF/MD of the lab protocols used.
- [ ] **Final Quality Report**: Comparative graphs showing the shift from *Tỏi Giống* to *Garlic 2.0*.
