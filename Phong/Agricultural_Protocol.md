# Agricultural Standard Operating Procedure (SOP): 2x2 Factorial Trial
**Project:** Garlic 2.0 | **Version:** 1.0 (ISO: 2026-04-14)
**Lead:** Phong | **Objective:** Precise execution of the NPK-Synergy trial.

---

## 1. Initial Setup & Soil Preparation
*Objective: Create a uniform, high-quality substrate for all experimental groups.*

1.  **Substrate Mix**: Blend potting soil as per local standards. **Phong to specify ratios of clay/sand/compost.**
2.  **Soil Testing**: Extract samples for the **Professional Lab OM Test** before any treatment.
3.  **Experimental Layout**: Mark 4 Blocks (Replications). Randomly assign Group IDs (C-, T1, T2, T3) to pots/plots within each block.
4.  **Basal Fertilization (Day -2)**:
    - Apply 33% of the Total Calculated Nitrogen (TCN) + 100% of P and K.
    - **TCN Calculation Required**: [Total kg/ha equivalent] converted to [grams/pot].

---

## 2. Planting & Inoculation
*Objective: Standardized planting depth and microbial delivery.*

1.  **Clove Selection**: Follow Nghĩa's **Step 0.2 (IDs assigned to each pot)**.
2.  **Planting Depth**: 5 cm depth, maintaining the 10cm x 30cm spacing rule from `Trial_Design.md`.
3.  **Inoculation Protocol (Phát Integration)**:
    - **Step A**: 30-min clove soak in microbial inoculant ($10^8$ CFU/mL) for T1 and T3.
    - **Step B**: 100mL soil drench immediately post-planting for T1 and T3.
4.  **Control groups (C- and T2)**: Apply 100mL sterile water/broth to maintain moisture parity.

---

## 3. Maintenance & Top-Dressing
*Objective: Timed nutrient delivery to match garlic phenology.*

| Week | Action | Specification |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Week 2** | Weed Management | Manual weeding only; avoid chemical herbicides. |
| **Week 4** | **Top-Dress #1** | Apply 33.5% of TCN. Apply **Soil Drench** of microbial culture (T1/T3). |
| **Week 8** | **Top-Dress #2** | Apply 33.5% of TCN (Remaining). Apply final **Soil Drench** (T1/T3). |
| **Growth** | Irrigation | Maintain field capacity; record any drainage loss. |

---

## 4. The "Stop Rule" (Pre-Harvest)
*Objective: Ensure chemical stability before analysis.*

1.  **Irrigation Stop**: Halt all irrigation 15 days before harvest to allow bulb maturation.
2.  **Foliar Indices**: Record final leaf yellowing (Senescence mapping) across all groups.

---

## 5. Harvest & Handover
*Objective: Secure transition to analysis.*

1.  **Extraction**: Carefully remove bulbs to minimize root damage.
2.  **Curing**: 3-day shade-drying before baseline physical weighing.
3.  **Handover**: Transfer samples to **Nghĩa** as per the [Task.md](file:///home/mashxp/Documents/MyVault/Atlas/Garlic/Ngh%C4%A9a/Task.md) ID registry.
