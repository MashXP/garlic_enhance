# Experimental Trial Design Protocol: Garlic (Allium Sativum L.)

This protocol standardizes the experimental design for garlic trials within the project, ensuring all fertilization and extraction research aligns with established scientific standards and the Project's **2x2 Factorial Synergy** model.

## 1. Experimental Architecture

### 1.1 Randomized Complete Block Design (RCBD)
To account for soil heterogeneity (gradients in N, moisture, or elevation), all field trials must utilize the **RCBD** framework.
- **Blocks (Replications):** Minimum 3 blocks; **4 blocks preferred** for yield data.
- **Randomization:** Treatments must be assigned to plots within each block randomly.

### 1.2 The 2x2 Factorial Synergy Design
The core goal is to test the synergy between chemical fertilization (Phong) and bio-targeted isolation (Phát).

| Group ID | Fertilizer ($F$) | Bacteria ($B$) | Hypothesis/Expectation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Control (C-)** | No Treatment | No Treatment | Baseline growth in sterile substrate. |
| **Fert-Only (T2)** | **Yes** | No | Expected growth boost from standard NPK. |
| **Bio-Only (T1)** | No | **Yes** | Expected boost from Phát's bacterial culture. |
| **Combined (T3)** | **Yes** | **Yes** | **Strongest Growth:** Synergistic $1+1 > 2$ effect. |

### 1.3 Responsibilities
- **Phong (Lead):** Trial infrastructure, soil sourcing, and "Yes Fert" (Standard NPK) management.
- **Phát (Microbiology):** Finding, culturing, and executing the inoculation of the "Yes Bacteria" groups.

---

## 2. Methodology & Plot Specifications

### 2.1 Plot Dimensions
Standardized from *Diriba-Shiferaw (2017)* benchmarking:
- **Plot Area:** 3.6 m² (1.8m x 2.0m).
- **Plant Spacing:** 10 cm; **Row Spacing:** 30 cm.
- **Buffers:** 1.0m between plots; 1.5m between blocks to prevent cross-contamination.

### 2.2 Sampling Logic ($n$)
Based on the *Zhang et al. (2023)* benchmark for statistical power:
- **Yield ($n \ge 6$):** Harvest 10 bulbs from center rows per block. Aggregate for plot mean.
- **Quality ($n \ge 3$):** 3-5 representative bulbs per block for composite HPLC/Assay analysis.

---

## 3. Synergy Analysis (The "Project Proof")

The trial is considered a success if the **Synergy Index (SI)** is positive:
$$SI = T3 - (T1 + T2 - C-)$$
- **If $SI > 0$:** We have proven that the specific bacteria isolated by Phát actively enhances the nutrient-use efficiency (NUE) of Phong's fertilization strategy.
